Outerwear

When in-between seasons incite the urge to layer, designer men's sweaters offer themselves as the perfect sartorial solution. Comprehensive in their variety and indiscriminate in their utility, sweaters are available for whatever the modern man might require. Crewneck and V-neck variations in subdued tones of fine gauge wool and cashmere complement classic tailoring, while vividly-hued jacquard patterns and bold intarsia graphics are a feast for the eyes. Artistic cable knits and rib knits in exaggerated proportions lend modern dimensions and textural interest. Unexpected design flourishes abound: turtlenecks are elevated effortlessly through a simplistic attention to detail.
